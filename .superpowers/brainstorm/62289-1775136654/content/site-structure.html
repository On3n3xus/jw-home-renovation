JW Home Renovation — Site Structure

Single-page scrolling site mirroring the Refit reference. All sections listed top to bottom.

Page Sections (scroll order)

1
Navigation — Fixed top bar. "JW Home Renovation" logo left, nav links right (About, Services, Our Work, FAQs, Contact). Transparent over hero, solid on scroll.
2
Hero — Dark bg, two-column. Left: "Available for work" badge + H1 headline + subtitle + "Work with us" CTA. Right: Large interior photo + floating testimonial card overlay.
3
About Us — White bg. Section pill + heading + description. Auto-scrolling image marquee. 4 animated stat counters (Years, Projects, Team, Satisfaction).
4
Services — Off-white bg. Two-column: left image (swaps per service), right accordion (6 services with icons, expand/collapse).
5
Our Work (Portfolio) — Cool gray bg. 3 stacked project cards with alternating light/dark backgrounds, each with photo + details + tags + client testimonial.
6
Testimonials — White bg. Two-row auto-scrolling marquee (opposite directions). Each card: stars + quote + avatar + name.
7
FAQs — White bg. Two-column: left heading + "Get in touch" CTA, right accordion with 7 questions.
8
Contact — Dark bg with rounded top corners. Two-column: left contact details (address, email, phone, socials), right contact form (Name, Email, Phone, Message + submit).
9
Footer — Dark bg. Logo + quick links columns + copyright bar.

Design System

Colors

Monochromatic: dark charcoal, white, soft grays

Typography

Manrope — Headings, nav, buttons, stat numbers
Host Grotesk — Body text, descriptions

Animations

Framer Motion: scroll reveals, counter animations, section transitions. CSS: infinite marquees (images + testimonials).

Tech Stack

Next.js 15 (App Router) + Tailwind CSS v4 + Framer Motion + TypeScript
