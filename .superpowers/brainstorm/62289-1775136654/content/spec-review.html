Page Sections (9 total)

1. Navigation Bar FIXED

Fixed top bar. "JW Home Renovation" logo left, nav links right (About, Services, Our Work, FAQs, Contact). Transparent over hero → solid on scroll with backdrop blur.

2. Hero Section DARK BG

Two-column: Left — "Available for work" badge, H1 "Your trusted partner for quality home improvement", subtitle, "Work with us" CTA. Right — large kitchen photo + floating testimonial card overlay. Staggered entrance animation.

3. About Us WHITE BG

Pill badge + heading "home improvement specialists" + description paragraph.

Image marquee: 6 renovation photos auto-scrolling horizontally (infinite loop, pause on hover).

Stats: 15+ years | 250+ projects | 30+ tradespeople | 100% satisfaction — animated counters on scroll.

4. Services OFF-WHITE BG

Two-column: Left image (swaps per active service), right accordion.

Kitchens Loft Conversions Bathrooms Extensions Restorations External Works
5. Portfolio (Our Work) LIGHT GRAY BG

3 stacked project cards with alternating backgrounds (white → dark → light gray). Each card: photo + title + description + category/duration tags + client testimonial quote + avatar.

1. Modern kitchen refit — Kitchen — 4 weeks — Rachel Morgan
2. External garden path build — External Works — 1 month — Michael Turner
3. Bathroom renovation — Bathroom — 6 weeks — Laura Davies
6. Testimonials WHITE BG

Two-row auto-scrolling marquee (opposite directions). Each card: 5 stars + quote + avatar + name. 6 placeholder testimonials. Pause on hover.

7. FAQs WHITE BG

Two-column: Left — heading + "Get in touch" CTA. Right — 7-item accordion (service area, timelines, quotes, permits, guarantees, staying home, getting started).

8. Contact DARK BG

Rounded top corners. Two-column: Left — address, email, phone, social icons. Right — contact form (Name*, Email*, Phone, Message*, "Send message" button). Static only — no backend.

9. Footer DARK BG

"JW Home Renovation" logo + quick links (2 columns) + copyright bar "© 2026 JW Home Renovation. All rights reserved."
